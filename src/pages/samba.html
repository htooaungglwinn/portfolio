The Goal

Catching viewers attention and communicating the visual connections

During my free time, I put my hands dirty doing fun collages or experimental graphic posters. As being an active fan of classic fashion, the samba is one of my favorite sneakers. So, I decided to do a project that reflects the look and feel of them.

The Grids

8x8 Grids Layout in use

After a Swiss design legend, Josef Muller Brookman’s book, “The Grid System In Graphic Design”, the poster uses an 8x8 grid layouts and also conveys the aesthetics use of typeface in the grid composition.

The Final Designs

Final Poster Designs

This was one of my first poster designs after reading and being influenced by Swiss Design and Grid Systems. Through this project, I was able to learn the importance of visual communication and appreciate the use of grids in a layout
